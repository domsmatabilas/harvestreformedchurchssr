Ps. David Micallef

Pastor / B.Th. ACT (Reformed Theological College)

David is married to Chrissy, together they have 4 children. Having studied at Reformed Theological College, graduating with a B.Th in 2021, David has been involved in various ministry in the Point Cook Area to local community.

Being a musician with a passion to help people, David has experience in roles such as teaching English language through song to the local community and music therapy in local nursing homes.

When David is not immersed in pastoring and teaching, he enjoys riding his motorcycle and gardening as a great means of “refilling his tank” so he is further able to devote his whole self to the living word of the living God!
